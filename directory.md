### Private Stacks & Enterprise Infrastructure

Beyond what is public, I maintain several proprietary environments and mission-critical stacks under ARPA. These include core platforms like **arpa.chat** and the **Synapuls** bio/neuro-suite, as well as various personal, enterprise, and government-level repositories. 

While these are kept private for security and confidentiality, I occasionally grant access to researchers and contributors who have a legitimate, well-defined reason to explore or build upon them. If you think your work aligns with these projects, you can reach out to me directly at **r1@arpacorp.net**.
